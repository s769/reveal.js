NERSC's Role

  • The entire end-to-end Bayesian inference and prediction framework was executed on Perlmutter.
  • The offline phase required over 70k node hours, granted through an ALCC and High-Impact Science at Scale allocations.
  • New NVIDIA libraries including cuDSS and cuSOLVERMp were utilized in the full-scale inversion/prediction.
  • Over 10TB of data was precomputed in the offline phase.
  • Development and testing of the real-time inversion framework was also done on Perlmutter.
  • Special thanks to NERSC staff (Amanda Dufek and Muaaz Awan) who were helpful for full-system scaling runs.

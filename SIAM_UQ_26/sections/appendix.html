Informative Subspace: Proof Sketch

To show $\text{span}\{u_k\} = \mathcal{N}(F_q)^\perp \cap \mathcal{H}_{\text{post}}$:

\[\footnotesize \begin{align*} \langle x, u_k \rangle_{\mathcal{H}_{\text{post}}} &= \langle x, \Gamma_{\!\text{post}} F_q^* v_k \rangle_{\mathcal{H}_{\text{post}}}\\ &= \langle \Gamma_{\!\text{post}}^{-1} x, \Gamma_{\!\text{post}} F_q^* v_k \rangle \\ &= \langle x, F_q^* v_k \rangle\\ &= \langle F_q x, v_k\rangle \end{align*} \]
  • If $\langle x, u_k \rangle_{\mathcal{H}_{\text{post}}} = 0$ for all $u_k$, then $\langle F_q x, v_k\rangle = 0$ for all $v_k$.
  • Since $\{v_k\}$ is a basis for $\mathcal{H}_q$, $F_q x = 0$, meaning $x \in \mathcal{N}(F_q)$.
  • Thus, $\text{span}(u_k) \subseteq \mathcal{N}(F_q)^\perp$.
  • By Hermitian symmetry, $\langle u_k, x \rangle = 0$ for $x \in \mathcal{N}(F_q) \cap \mathcal{H}_{\text{post}}$

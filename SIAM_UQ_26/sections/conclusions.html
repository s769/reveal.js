Conclusions

  • Presented theoretical and numerical framework for goal-oriented dimension reduction for Bayesian inverse problems
    • Readily applicable for linear autonomous dynamical systems
    • Can be extended to other classes of problems
  • Reduced space inverse problem cast in the QoI space rather than the data space
  • Large-scale application to tsunami early warning system
  • Next steps: use basis to train a surrogate model SWE to propagate tsunami waves

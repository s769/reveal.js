Contributions

  • Real-time Bayesian inference/prediction framework
    • Overall ideas came from group discussion with advisor and postdocs
    • I designed parts of the framework, such as avoiding prior solves in real-time and computing uncertainties in displacement field
    • I implemented the inversion and prediction framework at production scale, enabling the solution of an inverse/prediction problem with over 1 billion parameters and 250,000 data points in under 0.2 seconds (2025 ACM Gordon Bell Prize Finalist).
  • Fast Hessian matvec algorithm
    • I conceptualized the algorithm based on an initial group discussion
    • I implemented the algorithm from the ground up and optimized performance on GPUs across multiple hardware architectures
  • Massively scalable PDE solver
    • Implemented by Stefan and optimized with help from the MFEM team at LLNL
    • I helped with generating the computational meshes based on bathymetry data and preprocessing the seismic data to use in the simulation

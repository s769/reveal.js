Digital Twin for Tsunami Early Warning

  • Use near-field pressure observations to infer the seafloor motion and forward predict the tsunami propagation
  • Employ high-fidelity, full-physics model (acoustic–gravity eqns.)
  • Quantify uncertainty via Bayesian inference
  • Solve inverse problem in real time (order of seconds)
  • Challenges
    • Forward problem is 3D space + time; parameter is 2D space + time
    • Hyperbolic forward PDEs don't easily lend themselves to surrogates
    • Inverse problem is ill-posed: have to infer high-dimensional parameter field from sparse, noisy data
  • Additional outer-loop problems
    • Optimal sensor placement
    • Data-driven prior modeling
    • Coupling to elastic/shallow-water models

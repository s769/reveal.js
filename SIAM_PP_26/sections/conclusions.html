Conclusions

  • Developed and implemented scalable and efficient algorithms for optimal sensor placement for problems governed by linear autonomous dynamical systems
  • Standard and goal-oriented D-optimal design approaches can be used depending on the desired objective
  • FFT-based fast p2o/p2q applications enable direct access to dense operators
  • Penalty terms for sensor depth, cost constraints, etc., region masking, or other optimization algorithms can be easily incorporated into the framework
